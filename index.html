Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026  |  Effective Date: March 24, 2026

TxtProof ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Salveo Capital LLC, is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their clients. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information in connection with the TxtProof SMS archiving platform ("the Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business information provided during registration.
  • SMS Message Content: The content of SMS messages sent to and from your TxtProof number, including sender/recipient phone numbers, message body, and timestamp.
  • Phone Numbers: Mobile phone numbers of end-users who send messages to your TxtProof number, treated as personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Usage Data: Log data and device information related to your use of the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • To forward SMS messages to your designated phone number
  • To archive message records and deliver them to your designated email address
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support

3. SMS Data and Client Communications

TxtProof processes SMS message content solely to provide the archiving and forwarding service. Message content is transmitted via Twilio's messaging infrastructure. We do not use message content for marketing or any purpose beyond delivering the Service.

Advisors are responsible for obtaining all required consents from their clients prior to SMS communication, including consents required under FINRA, SEC, and TCPA regulations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information. We share information only with service providers necessary to operate the Service, including Twilio (SMS infrastructure) and cloud hosting providers. We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.

5. Data Retention

Archived message records are delivered to your designated email address. TxtProof retains message logs for a minimum of 6 years to assist with SEC and FINRA recordkeeping requirements, unless otherwise required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical measures to protect your information. All data is transmitted using encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS).

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us below. Certain data may be subject to regulatory retention obligations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
